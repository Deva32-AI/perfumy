Perfume is a fragrant liquid made from natural or synthetic aromatic ingredients that give a pleasant smell. It helps people feel fresh, confident, and attractive throughout the day. Perfume is commonly used as part of daily grooming and personal care.

Different perfumes have different scent types such as floral, woody, fruity, spicy, and fresh. Each fragrance creates a unique mood and impression. Choosing the right perfume depends on personal taste, occasion, and season.

Perfume also reflects personality and style. A good-quality perfume lasts longer and leaves a memorable impression. Today, perfumes are available in many varieties for men and women, making them an important part of modern lifestyle and fashion.
